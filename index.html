What is Generative AI?

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that can create new content such as images, text, audio, and video based on patterns learned from large datasets.

It is widely used in design, education, media production, and software development.

Advantages

  • Fast content creation
  • Reduces time and effort
  • Supports creativity and idea generation
  • Used in many industries

Disadvantages

  • May produce inaccurate results
  • Can replace some human jobs
  • Depends on data quality
  • Ethical concerns
